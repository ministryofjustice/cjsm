Access Keys

Access keys are keyboard shortcuts that help users who have difficulty using pointing devices such as a mouse. This website uses the UK government access keys standards. Below is a description of the access keys that are used on this site:

Depending upon your browser use the shortcuts in the following ways:

  • Microsoft Internet Explorer 4 - hold down alt and the letter/number at the same time
  • Microsoft Internet Explorer 5 and above - hold down alt and the letter/number at the same time, then press the enter key
  • Netscape 6 and above - hold down alt and the letter/number at the same time
