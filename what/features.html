What is CJSM for?

Features

After looking at the way people use their existing email, and talking to users about what functions they wanted CJSM to have, we've made a number of improvements to both webmail and server accounts to make CJSM easier and simpler to use.

Already using CJSM? Discover the latest changes
Server Features


Does your address look like this? peter.pan@org.co.uk.cjsm.net

Having a server-based account means that you connect to the CJSM service via SMTP. You will continue to use your existing email account, but we will simply add the suffix 'cjsm.net' to your address for CJSMs.
Webmail Features


Does your address look like this? peter.pan@org.cjsm.net

Having a webmail account means that you connect to the CJSM service via your internet browser.
