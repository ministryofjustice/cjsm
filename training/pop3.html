Connecting to CJSM via POP3

Notes

Please note you can only use POP3 if you are using an email client on your device, e.g. Microsoft Outlook, Thunderbird, MacMail - you cannot use POP3 with another online webmail service e.g. Gmail, etc.

CJSM mailboxes can accessed in one of two ways - via webmail or alternatively it can be delivered into your normal email client, e.g. Microsoft Outlook, Mozilla Thunderbird, MacMail, etc. This uses an email protocol known as Post Office Protocol 3 or POP3 for short. CJSM email will usually show as another folder in your email client.

If you would like to receive your CJSM email into your normal email client please select from the instruction guides below and follow the steps in the document.

Please note if you are using Windows 8.1 or 10 you may have an additional step, please follow all the instructions in the document.

If any doubt please contact the CJSM Helpdesk on 0870 0108535 / 020 7604 5598.
