Signing Up

It only takes a few minutes to sign up for the CJSM service. If you’re ready to sign up now, Apply Now to go straight to our simple, user-friendly online application.

If you’re already using CJSM and you'd like to introduce more of your colleagues to the service, you can Tell a Friend. If you choose this option, we will email your chosen colleague(s) an invitation to join the CJSM service, together with a little bit of information about the benefits of using it.
