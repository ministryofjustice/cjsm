Welcome to CJSM

Secure, seamless communication across the justice community

What is CJSM?

CJSM is much more than a secure email service for the justice system.

It also offers:

  • an onboarding and assurance process
  • the most complete directory of justice professionals
  • a direct connection to secure government networks
  • administration tools
  • a dedicated helpdesk

And it's free for registered users.

Learn more about CJSM >

Who is CJSM for?

CJSM is for practitioners involved in any aspect of the justice system.

This includes:

  • Government departments
  • Legal representatives
  • Police services
  • Other investigating and prosecuting bodies
  • Local government
  • Healthcare
  • Support and advocacy services
  • Multi-agency groups
